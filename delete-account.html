Delete Your PathBinder Account

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This page explains how to delete your PathBinder account and the personal data associated with it. PathBinder is operated by PathBinder LLC (pathbinder.gg and the PathBinder mobile app).

How to delete your account

In the app or on the web: sign in, then go to Account → Settings → Delete Account and confirm. Your account is scheduled for deletion with a 30-day grace period — sign back in any time within 30 days to cancel it. After that, your profile, photos, and personal data are permanently removed.

If you can’t sign in: email support@pathbinder.gg from the email address on your account and ask us to delete it. We’ll verify it’s you and complete the request, typically within 30 days.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes the following from our active systems:

  • Your profile — name, username, avatar, bio, shop details, and social links
  • Your collection and binders, including per-item data and photos you uploaded
  • Your marketplace listings
  • Your messages, seller ratings, and reviews
  • Your wishlist, price alerts, and preferences
  • Your account credentials and contact details

What is kept, and for how long

We retain a limited set of records only where the law requires, or to complete open transactions, resolve disputes, and prevent fraud — for example, completed order and transaction records (amounts, dates, and order status). These are kept for as long as required by applicable tax, accounting, and legal-compliance obligations (in the United States, financial records are commonly retained for up to 7 years), after which they are deleted.

PathBinder never stores your payment card numbers — those are handled entirely by our payment processor, Stripe, and are not part of the data we hold or retain.

Questions

If you have any questions about account deletion or your data, contact us at support@pathbinder.gg. See our Privacy Policy for full details on how we handle your information.
