## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They are widely used to make websites work, remember your preferences, and provide information to site owners.

"Similar technologies" includes localStorage, sessionStorage, IndexedDB, web beacons, pixel tags, and software development kits (SDKs) embedded in our mobile applications.

This Policy covers all of the above; for brevity we use the word "cookies" throughout.

## 4. Managing Your Cookies

### 4.1 Browser Controls

Most browsers let you refuse or delete cookies through settings. Note that disabling strictly-necessary cookies will prevent you from signing in or completing transactions.

- [Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)
- [Firefox](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)
- [Safari (Mac)](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)
- [Safari (iOS)](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT201265)
- [Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

### 4.2 Clearing PathBinder Storage

To clear PathBinder-specific data in your browser:

1. Sign out of your account (this clears localStorage avatar / badges / preferences).
2. In your browser's developer tools, open **Application → Storage → Clear site data**.

### 4.3 Do Not Track

Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" signal. We **[do / do not]** currently honor DNT signals because there is no industry-wide consensus on how to interpret them. We will update this Policy if that changes.

### 4.4 Cookie Consent Banner

For visitors from the EU, UK, and California, we display a cookie consent banner on first visit. You may withdraw consent at any time by:

- Clicking **[Cookie Settings link in footer]**, or
- Clearing your site data and revisiting (the banner reappears on next visit).
